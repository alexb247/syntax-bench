Baselines are shown muted: they carry no grammatical knowledge and exist as reference points. The vertical tick on each bar marks 50%, the score of pure chance.
Red flags mark a result a no-syntax heuristic could reach (the items are gameable by surface cues), or a large gap between the two presentation orders (the model is judging position, not grammar).
